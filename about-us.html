Our Journey

The Mandakini Story

From humble beginnings to becoming one of India's most innovative event management companies.

2015

Mandakini was born from a simple idea: events should be as unique as the people they celebrate. Our founders, with backgrounds in design, hospitality, and marketing, came together with a shared vision of transforming India's event landscape.

2017

After successfully executing over 50 corporate and social events, we expanded our team and services to include brand activations and campus events, bringing fresh energy and innovative concepts to younger audiences.

2019

Recognizing the growing demand for personalized experiences, we launched our corporate gifting division and sangeet choreography services, offering clients a truly end-to-end event solution.

2021

During the pandemic, we pivoted to virtual and hybrid events, developing innovative digital solutions that kept people connected when they couldn't be together physically.

2025

Today, Mandakini stands as a leader in the event industry, with a portfolio of over 500 successful events, a team of 30+ creative professionals, and a reputation for excellence that speaks for itself.
