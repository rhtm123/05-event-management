Frequently Asked Questions

An interior designer is a professional or expert with a trained eye in space planning, colour, texture, and how to make a space purposeful and beautiful that perfectly suits the lifestyle of the users.

An interior designer can analyze the space with the client's goals in mind and turn it into an eye-pleasing and functional area. They can prepare concepts, schematics, drawings & sketches, help with project estimates, scheduling, and organizing materials flow, saving you time and money.

An interior designer can improve your space by making it better suited to its purpose. They can maximize storage, help with closets, kitchens, bathrooms, and other spaces. Designers also have access to suitable vendors for furniture, lighting, flooring, fabrics, and hardware, bringing everything together for a cohesive flow throughout the house.
